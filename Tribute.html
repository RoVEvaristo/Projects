MICHAEL JACKSON

Michael Jackson was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer, dubbed the "King of Pop". He was known for his influential contributions to music, dance, and fashion, as well as his publicized personal life. With his songs like Billie Jean, Beat it, Thriller, and many more, he made a huge impact in the music industry by giving soul and life to his music. He was an icon back then and still remains to be today. He will forever be part of world's greatest musicians.
